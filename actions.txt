1. Return
  - Return up to X cards to their owners hands when invoking this creature
  - usage: "return <X>"
2. Double Breaker
  - Breaks 2 shields when attacking shields
  - usage: "doublebreaker"
3. Power Attacker
  - Gets +X attack while attacking
  - usage: "powerattacker <X>"
4. Can't be blocked
  - usage: "noblock"
5. Destroy All Blockers
  - usage: "destroyBlockers"
6. Can be untapped
  - At the end of each of your turns, you may untap this creature.
  - usage: "canBeUntapped"
